Berry Bannana Recipe!

To create the Berry Bannana Smoothie Bowl, you will need the following ingredients

  • 1 Banana
  • 2 tbs of blueberries
  • 2 tbs of strawberries
  • 1 tbs of raspberries
  • 200 ml of oatmilk (your choice of milk)
  • 1 scoop of Whey Protein
  • 1 drop of Vanilla Extract

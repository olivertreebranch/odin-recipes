Strawberry Bannana Recipe!

To create the Strawberry Smoothie Bowl, you will need the following ingredients

  • 2 cups organic frozen strawberries

    • Blending Instructions

    1. Add the strawberries and milk to your blender.
    2. Start speed on low.
    3. Push frozen fruit down to the blades.
    4. Keep blending until there are no frozen fruit chunks left.
    5. Scoop into a bowl.
    6. Add toppings.
    7. Enjoy!!
